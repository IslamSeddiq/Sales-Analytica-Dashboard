🧠 About the Project
The Sales Analytics Dashboard is a high-impact, executive-level business intelligence tool designed to track sales performance, evaluate profitability, and uncover product concentration risks. Built with a modern, dark-themed UI and striking red accents, the dashboard visualizes over $8.65M in total sales across three years (2023–2025).

Beyond standard charting, it features automated, dynamic insight panels that analyze performance distributions and instantly flag specific optimization areas—such as low-performing categories ripe for targeted promotions or high-revenue skew across product sub-categories.

💡 Business Value
This dashboard helps decision-makers to:

Track Profitability Health: Monitor profit margins closely alongside total sales volume to ensure growth remains profitable.

Formulate Promo Strategies: Pinpoint low-share categories (like Office Supplies at 7.55% of sales) to drive targeted marketing campaigns.

Manage Product Risks: Identify high-reliance risk areas (e.g., recognizing when only 4 sub-categories exceed $1M while 9 generate less than $100K each).

Optimize Regional Allocation: Make data-backed inventory and distribution decisions by comparing regional demand patterns.

Drive Faster Decisions: Eliminate guesswork with pre-calculated, screen-ready KPI summaries and trend charts.
